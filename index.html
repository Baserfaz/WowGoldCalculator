Gold calculator

Purpose

This application is meant to help maximizing the gold per hour (gph) when farming in World of Warcraft. The idea is to farm/grind in a same spot for some amount of time and then use this calculator to automatically calculate the gph. From the calculated gph you can compare farming spots.


How to

To use this application, give the starting amount of money you currently have on your character. After clicking the "Start timer"-button, you can start farming. After you are done, give the amount of money again and click "calculate"-button (it will show after clicking "start timer"-button). You should see calculated values in the table below.


Important pointers

Remember to always put the amount of gold, silver and copper that are in your inventory, don't use self calculated values. Example: you start with 100g 0s 0c and end with 125g 54s 12c. You simple put those two values in this calculator as start and end values.
